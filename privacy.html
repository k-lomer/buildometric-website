Lawful bases and data protection rights

Under UK data protection law, we must have a “lawful basis” for collecting and using your personal information. You can find out more about lawful bases on the ICO's website.

Your data protection rights (summary)

  • Right of access - request copies of your personal information.
  • Right to rectification - ask us to correct or complete inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Right to erasure - ask us to delete your personal information.
  • Right to restriction of processing - limit how we use your information.
  • Right to object - object to certain types of processing.
  • Right to data portability - request transfer of your personal information.
  • Right to withdraw consent - if consent is our basis, you can withdraw it anytime.

If you make a request, we must respond without undue delay and within one month. To make a request, contact us using the details above.

For providing services and goods:
